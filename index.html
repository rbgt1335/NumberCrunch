The games are named by how many moves the fastest solution is. So The Daily 5, for instance, can't be solved in any less than 5 moves, but can be solved ideally in 5 moves.

How is my data stored?

Your progress is saved locally in your browser similar to other daily games. This means if you clear your browser history your progress will be lost.

What is x² and √x?

x² will square your current value, or multiply it by itself. For instance, 3²=9 and 4²=16. √x will do the opposite, √16=4 and √9=3. Something to note is that 3 and 4 are right next to each other, but 9 and 16 are not. It's true that the farther you move up the number line the longer the gap is between rootable values.
