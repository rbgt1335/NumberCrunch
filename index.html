How To Play

Every day a new puzzle will be given out. The optimal solution is between 5 to 7 moves.

Friday's are "Free For All" in which the numbers are truly randomly generated and not guranteed to have a quick solution.

Helpful Hints:

Multiplying by 10 and dividing by 2 is equaivalent to multiplying by 5.

For substantial gaps using squares or roots will be fastest.

5000 and 5100 may be 100 away from one another... but 50 and 51 are one from one another.
