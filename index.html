New Puzzles Daily!

The Daily 6 is a puzzle that requires at most 6 moves to solve. It is possible that some may take less than 6 moves because, well, numbers do cool things!

The Daily Free For All is a puzzle that has a randomly generated target and is NOT guaranteed to have a close solution. Compete for the best score!

Some Hints

Multiplying by 10 and dividing by 2 is equaivalent to multiplying by 5.

For substantial gaps using squares or roots will be fastest, but rootable values get farther apart the higher you are.

5000 and 5100 may be 100 away from one another... but 50 and 51 are one from one another.

How is my data stored?

Your progress is saved locally in your browser similar to other daily games. This means if you clear your browser history your progress will be lost.
